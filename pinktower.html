Spring Newsletter

Spring has sprung at Aria

Everyone has Spring Fever at Aria as we start our countdown to Summer!  With only 9 weeks to go until the end of another amazing year at Aria Montessori School, we are aiming to fill each week with fun and exciting treats for the kids to enjoy.

This week we will be having our Annual Easter Egg hunt on Wednesday.  Bunny ears are welcome but not mandatory!  We will spend the day having lots of Easter fun.

 

Next week we will be heading on our last field trip of the year.  The permission slip is attached to this email.

also.

 

On Tuesday 23rd we will be having our Graduation picture Day.  This is mainly for graduating students who are heading to Kindergarten.  If you wish your child to take pictures please speak to Miss Salazar before Tuesday and we will make arrangements for this to happen.  Please note:  Your child will not be sent for pictures unless they are going to kindergarten or you have asked Miss Salazar.

 

What's coming up....

Summer Camp is drawing close and we have lots and lots of fun activities planned.  Don't forget that all payments need to be in on May1st .  We are booking up fast so please let us know your schedule so that we can start putting our classes together.  Please remember that ALL students attending Aria in September MUST be registered onto at least one summer camp.

May 10th is Mothers Day.

 

If all ladies could arrive no later than 8,30am we will serve you coffee and treats on the patio and then proceed one class at a time into the rooms for a short presentation.  This is a female only event, but if you cant make it an need to send a replacement please speak to myself or Miss Salazar prior to the day.

May 24th is Spirit Day at Aria.

 

We will celebrating all things Aria and giving our friends who are graduating a chance to wear their new school colors. All graduating students can wear a t-shirt from their new school and all returning Aria students will wear Aria t-shirts.  We will be learning about Aria throughout the 106 years that we have been here.  Its going to be such a fun day.

May 1st Is Earth Day at Aria.

 

Please have your child wear green and send them to school with either seeds, small outdoor plants, small plant pots. bedding soil or bedding plants.  We will spend the day planting and weeding and learning how we can take better care of our planet.

May 16th is Open House.

 

This is for registered families only.  It is a time for our students to show their parents just how much they have learned during this school year.  School will close early at 4.30pm and Open House starts at 5pm -6pm.  Siblings aren't encouraged to come so that you can focus on your student, but if you aren't able to `get a babysitter we will have a move playing in the office.

May 30th is our Unicorn and Super Hero Carnival.

 

All students can come dressed in their favorite Super Hero t-shirt or Unicorn clothes.  We will have a fun day of games, treats, face painting and surprises.  It will be a awesome time and a great way to celebrate a wonderful year at Aria.

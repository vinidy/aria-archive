Admission's Policy

 

» We enroll children between the ages of 2 years and 6 years . Your child must be toilet trained before starting school ,however, we do understand when a children have a “potty accident" and we ask you to provide a change of clothes so that they are able to be made comfortable

 

» On the third Monday in September you are invited to telephone the school to book a scheduled tour. We invite parents to view our school on an individual family basis. This allows parents the opportunity to ask questions and receive a one on one detailed tour of the school and its Montessori program. You will observe the children working within the classroom environment with the Montessori material.

 

» In order to be placed on the waiting list, we require you to have toured the school. After the tour has taken place you will be invited to complete an application and join the waiting list for the following year.

 

» Once you have returned your application with a $100 application fee, your child will be placed on the waiting list. The waiting list is on a first come first served basis.

 

» Siblings of a registered children are given priority on the wait list. They are required to be at least 2 years old to start school and toilet trained.

 

» Your application will be confirmed to you in March. You will receive a call from school confirming your child’s acceptance.

 

Thank you for selecting Aria Montessori School. Please call if you have any questions.
